Welcome to Sipoo Wolf Icehockey club

Sipoo Wolf is growing and welcomes both beginner and experienced players to our new and existing ice hockey teams. Every player is important to us. We aim to nurture and guide players towards a healthy lifestyle within our club. Alongside the development of hockey skills, we also consider the development of social skills to be equally important. Through training, we hope that every Wolf player will also learn to respect the game, opponents, referees, and coaches. We want to offer every child and young person the opportunity to engage in hockey, regardless of their age and skill level.
